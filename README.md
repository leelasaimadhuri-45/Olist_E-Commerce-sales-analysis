# Olist_E-Commerce-sales-analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Brazilian Olist e-commerce dataset to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and product category revenue trends. The goal is to generate business insights that help improve sales strategies and product performance.

The analysis was performed using **SQL for data querying** and **Power BI for dashboard visualization**.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- SQL
- Power BI
- Data Cleaning
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

---

## Dataset
Dataset Source: Kaggle  
Dataset Name: **Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist**

The dataset contains information about:

- Orders
- Customers
- Payments
- Products
- Reviews
- Sellers
- Order Items

---

## Data Analysis Workflow

### 1 Data Preparation
Data was cleaned and structured before analysis.

Steps included:
- Removing duplicates
- Handling missing values
- Joining multiple tables
- Creating analytical datasets

---

### 2 SQL Analysis
SQL queries were used to answer business questions such as:

- Total revenue generated
- Top performing product categories
- Revenue by state
- Payment method distribution
- Monthly revenue trends

Example analysis queries:
- Revenue by product category
- Top 10 products by revenue
- Revenue by payment method
- Customer distribution by region

---

### 3 Power BI Dashboard
An interactive dashboard was created with **three pages**.

---

## Dashboard Structure

### Page 1 — Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of sales performance.

KPIs:
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Average Review Score

Visualizations:
- Revenue by payment method
- Revenue by state
- Monthly revenue trend
- Top product categories

---

### Page 2 — Customer & Review Analysis
Focuses on customer distribution and satisfaction.

KPIs:
- Total Customers
- Revenue per Customer
- Average Review Score
- Repeat Customers

Visualizations:
- Customer distribution by state
- Revenue contribution by customer
- Review score distribution
- Revenue by review score
- Monthly customer growth

---

### Page 3 — Product & Category Performance
Analyzes product performance and category revenue.

KPIs:
- Total Customers
- Total Orders
- Total Products Sold

Visualizations:
- Revenue by product category
- Top 10 revenue generating products
- Average order value by category
- Product category comparison

---

## Key Insights

- Credit cards are the most common payment method.
- A few product categories generate the majority of revenue.
- Revenue shows steady growth over time.
- Higher review scores are associated with higher revenue.

---

## Business Value

This dashboard helps businesses:

- Identify top performing products
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Optimize product category strategies
- Improve customer satisfaction
